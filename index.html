Steve Harrington is a fictional character from the Netflix television show Stranger Things, portrayed by Joe Keery. While starting out as a typical unlikable jock, Steve has grown into a more protecting and caring character as the show has progressed, a development that has received widespread acclaim from critics and fans alike and has led to him becoming one of the show’s most beloved and enduring characters. Initially a part of the recurring cast, Keery was promoted to the main cast in the second season. Steve is a total sweetheart turned protector of the kids.

Fictional Character Biography

Season 1

Steve is initially portrayed as a stereotypical 1980s popular jock, antagonistic and unlikable. Steve had been dating Nancy Wheeler and is resentful when she begins spending time with Jonathan. Steve even takes part in breaking Jonathan’s camera. Nancy seems to become close to Jonathan, but Jonathan beats Steve in a fight after he calls her names. After realizing Nancy may be in danger, Steve tries to apologize and ends up fighting the Demogorgon with Nancy and Jonathan. After this, the three form a friendship.

Season 2

In Season 2, Steve’s relationship with Nancy is strained, and he breaks up with her after she will not say she loves him. His popularity fades. Steve also finds himself at odds with Billy Hargrove, a new student at the school who seeks to belittle him as “king.” Steve becomes involved with Mike Wheeler and his friends after Dustin reveals he found a small creature from the Upside Down. Steve grows close with Dustin and teaches him about self-confidence. Steve protects the children as Demodogs tear through Hawkins, putting himself in harm’s way. He becomes a fan favorite for his older-brother-style relationship with Dustin, and his iconic encounters before Mike in a battle to disable the monster.
